ABOUT US

TFI is a non-government organization ('NGO') with the primary objective of promoting education amongst Nigerian Children(Nursery to Secondary) and supporting Orphanages/care homes in Nigeria.TFI will partner with schools.organizations and individuals to provide primary to secondary education to children whose parents/caretakers cannot afford to by themselves.TFI will also support orphanages/carehomes by providing them with everyday basic needs i.e. provisions toiletries etc.
